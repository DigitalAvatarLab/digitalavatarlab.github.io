Our lab conducts cutting-edge research at the intersection of Computer Graphics, Computer Vision, and Artificial Intelligence. We focus on developing next-generation visual computing technologies that bridge the physical and digital worlds, enabling realistic and immersive digital experiences.

Our research centers on AI-based digital avatar creation, including expressive facial animation and high-fidelity virtual character generation. By integrating deep learning with advanced graphics techniques, we aim to create realistic and natural digital avatars.
